Submitted form data is transmitted to our CRM via an encrypted webhook and stored securely. We retain enquiry data for a maximum of 24 months or until you request deletion.

Your rights under POPIA

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013, you have the right to:

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@grdbase.co.za. We will respond within 30 days.
